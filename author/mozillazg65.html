[linux]配置 vsftp 启用被动模式（passive mode）解决客户端"no route to host"错误

Date Tue 20 May 2014 Category linux Tags linux / vsftp

系统: CentOS

  • 修改 vsftpd.conf，启用被动模式，指定端口:

    pasv_min_port=12000
pasv_max_port=12199
pasv_enable=YES

  • 配置防火墙 iptables，允许端口访问:

    iptables -I INPUT -p tcp --dport 12000:12199 -j ACCEPT
service iptables save

[英语]other、others 以及 another 三者的区别

Date Thu 24 April 2014 Category english Tags english / 英语

看一下下面三个例句就知道它们之间的区别了：

  • There are three books on my table, One is here. Where are other books?
  • There are three books on my table, One is here. Where are the others?
