Cyanide & Happiness #3996：伤痛

2015-07-24

comics Tags Cyanide and Happiness

译自：Cyanide & Happiness #3996 - Explosm.net

hey, dad.
嗨，爸爸。

i've been thinking a lot since you've been gone. i guess i just wanted to tell you what's on my mind...
自从你去世后，我想了很多。 我只是想告诉你我是怎么想的。。。

mom says that you killed yourself because you were in a lot of emotional pain.
妈妈说你是自杀的，因为你陷入了严重的情感伤痛。

she said you left use cause you didn't want anyone to see you hurt.
她说你离开我们是因为你不想让任何人看到你受伤。

i wanted to tell you that you didn't stop the pain. you gave it to all of us.
我想告诉你的是，你并没有终结伤痛。 你把伤痛留给了我们大家。
