[英语]other、others 以及 another 三者的区别

Date Thu 24 April 2014 Category english Tags english / 英语

看一下下面三个例句就知道它们之间的区别了：

  • There are three books on my table, One is here. Where are other books?
  • There are three books on my table, One is here. Where are the others?
  • There are three books on my table, Two is here. Where are another?

[英语]data or datas

Date Mon 23 September 2013 Category english Tags english / 英语

data is right, datas is wrong.

If you have some data in one file and then some more in another, that is not two data or two datas; it is just data.
